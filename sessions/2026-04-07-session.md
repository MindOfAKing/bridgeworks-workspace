## Decisions made
- Server/client component split pattern chosen over generateMetadata-in-layout for cleaner per-page SEO
- In-memory rate limiting chosen over Redis (sufficient for launch volume)
- Honeypot spam protection chosen over CAPTCHA (no third-party dependency, invisible to users)
- Contact API logs submissions to console; email delivery (Resend/SendGrid) deferred to integration phase
