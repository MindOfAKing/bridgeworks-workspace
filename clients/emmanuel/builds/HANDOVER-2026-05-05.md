## Brand fidelity reference

If you make changes, the brand contract is in `world-model/clients/emmanuel/brand-system/`:
- `tokens.json` — machine-readable brand tokens (colors, type scale, motion, spacing)
- `guidelines.md` — engine-readable distillation of the canonical PDFs
- `logos/` — wordmark SVGs (dark + light variants, mirrored from `world-model/org/brand/logos/`)

The canonical source PDFs at `world-model/org/brand/emmanuel/` are founder-only. Engine reads, never modifies.

## Voice rules (apply to ALL copy edits)

- No em dashes anywhere
- No AI slop vocabulary: cutting-edge, innovative, seamless, ecosystem, transformative, robust, world-class, passionate about, leverage, synergize, industry-leading
- Short sentences, one idea per sentence
- Specific over general (real numbers, real timelines)
- First-person where natural
- Faith as natural context, never as performance
- Honest about difficulty (kitchen shifts, paperwork, cash crunch — don't smooth)
