## Key Observations

1. Scale is strong — nearly 40k profile views. This is a well-discovered listing.
2. All interaction metrics are declining Jan–Jun. Profile is coasting without active management.
3. "restaurants" (14k) is the #1 trigger — generic discovery, not branded intent.
4. "afrikai étterem budapest" (684) shows strong Hungarian-language search demand.
5. "olivia" (728) is a brand confusion signal — people finding Oliviks while searching for Olivia.
6. Menu views have dropped 75% since January — likely linked to no menu photos and no GBP menu entries.
7. Bookings collapsed to near zero — bookings feature may not be set up correctly.
8. Description was weak and generic before today's update.
