Founder story

Cynthia and Aese built Oliviks after Debrecen.

Cynthia and Aese are a Nigerian couple who studied for their Masters in Debrecen before creating Oliviks Kitchen. They saw how few places in Hungary served the Nigerian food they knew from home, then built a Budapest kitchen for Nigerians, expats, and local guests discovering jollof, egusi, suya, puff puff, and more.
