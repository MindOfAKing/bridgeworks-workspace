# Oliviks — Weekly Specials Template (Foundation A3, deliverable #4)

A fill-in-the-blank template for the weekly specials email, in English and Hungarian.
Keep it short. One or two specials, one photo each, one link. Send the same content as
a WhatsApp broadcast (see whatsapp-broadcast-template.md). Swap the [brackets] each week.

---

## ENGLISH

**Subject:** This week at Oliviks: [dish or theme]
**Preview text:** [one-line hook, e.g. "Egusi is back, and there is jollof for the table."]

Hi [first name / there],

This week at the kitchen:

**[Dish name] — [price] Ft**
[One or two sentences. What it is, why it is good this week.]

**[Optional second dish] — [price] Ft**
[One sentence.]

Order for pickup at Rákóczi tér 9 → [shop.oliviks.com]
Or find us on Wolt and Marwa.

Come hungry.
Cynthia & Aese, Oliviks Kitchen
Mon–Sat 11:00–20:00

---

## HUNGARIAN (Magyar)

**Tárgy:** A hét az Oliviksnál: [étel vagy téma]
**Előnézeti szöveg:** [egysoros horog]

Szia [keresztnév],

Ezen a héten a konyhánkban:

**[Étel neve] — [ár] Ft**
[Egy-két mondat. Mi ez, miért jó ezen a héten.]

**[Opcionális második étel] — [ár] Ft**
[Egy mondat.]

Rendeld elvitelre a Rákóczi tér 9 alatt → [shop.oliviks.com]
Vagy keress minket a Wolton és a Marwán.

Gyere éhesen.
Cynthia és Aese, Oliviks Kitchen
Hétfő–szombat 11:00–20:00

---

**Weekly workflow (5 minutes):** pick the dish, drop in the price, add one photo,
send to the email list, then paste the same into the WhatsApp broadcast. Best send
time for a lunch/dinner restaurant: Thursday or Friday late morning.
