# Oliviks — Welcome Email Sequence (Foundation A3, deliverable #3)

Three automated emails that go out after someone joins the list. Load these into
MailerLite/Beehiiv as an automation triggered on "subscriber confirmed" (double opt-in).
Brand voice: short sentences, warm, specific. No AI slop. From: Oliviks Kitchen.

Incentive referenced: **Free puff puff with your first pickup order** (confirm final
wording + how it is redeemed with Aese before launch — e.g. show the email at the
counter, or a code).

## Email 2 — The founders' story (sends 2 days later)

**Subject:** Why we opened a Nigerian kitchen in Budapest
**Preview text:** We could not find the food we grew up on. So we cooked it.

Hi again,

A quick story about why Oliviks exists.

We came to Hungary to study. Both of us did our Masters in Debrecen, far from Lagos
and far from the food we grew up on. Real Nigerian cooking was not on the menu
anywhere. Not close.

So we cooked it ourselves. Oliviks is a Nigerian kitchen, not an African-themed one.
The recipes come from Cynthia's family table, made from scratch, seasoned the way they
should be.

Budapest answered. Nigerians looking for home, Hungarians trying egusi for the first
time, students, regulars who learned the menu and never left it. Most order again.
That is the whole point.

If you have not claimed your free puff puff yet, it is still waiting.

Order for pickup → [shop.oliviks.com]

Cynthia & Aese
