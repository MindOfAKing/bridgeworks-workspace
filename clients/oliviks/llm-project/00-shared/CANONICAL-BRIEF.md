# Canonical Brief

## One-Line Positioning

Oliviks Kitchen is a Nigerian kitchen in Budapest built by Cynthia and Aese, serving real Nigerian comfort food from Rakoczi ter 9 with strong customer proof and press credibility.

## Brand Direction

Warm. Bold. Food-first. Founder-led. Specific. Proud without overexplaining.

## Commercial Direction

The website should push direct ordering first.

Order priority:
1. Direct website order
2. WhatsApp or phone
3. Visit/pickup
4. Wolt and Foodora as secondary delivery/discovery options

## Do Not Use

- Organic store positioning
- Pesticide-free language
- Wellness or clean-eating claims
- Generic "authentic experience"
- Generic restaurant copy
- Fake testimonials
- Random African pattern decoration
- Generic stock portraits
- Wolt/Foodora as primary CTA

## Open Items To Verify

- Exact 23 menu products
- Exact product prices
- Exact current opening hours
- Exact Foodora listing URL
- Google Business Profile access
- WordPress/WooCommerce access
- Direct ordering backend decision
- Real founder photos and food photos
- Existing review excerpts/screenshots
