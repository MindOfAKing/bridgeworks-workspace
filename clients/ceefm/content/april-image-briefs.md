**Post 7 (Apr 15) -- Hotel Housekeeping Standards:** Split image: pristine hotel bathroom mirror reflection + perfectly made bed linens. Clean, bright photography.

**Post 8 (Apr 16) -- Student Housing FM:** Photo of a modern student housing common area or study room. Clean, functional design.
