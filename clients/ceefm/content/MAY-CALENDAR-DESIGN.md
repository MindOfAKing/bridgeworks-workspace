## Hard Constraints (locked, do not violate)

1. **No internal-process content.** No posts about "how we rebuilt CEEFM's brand voice" or other operations-of-the-engagement reveals. The public sees the output, not the internal mechanics.
2. **No Limehome attribution.** Until Victor confirms permission in writing, all case-study content must be anonymized ("a 120-room Budapest aparthotel", "a 50-unit residential building"). No client names, no identifying property addresses.
3. **No public floor pricing.** May Action Plan flagged this as a Victor decision; default until approved is no price disclosure on LinkedIn.

## Format

- **Hungarian:** with proper diacritics (á, é, í, ó, ö, ő, ú, ü, ű). April was diacritic-free for platform compatibility; May upgrades the standard.
- **Brand colors in image briefs:** Forest Green #1C3D2A, Sage Green #8FBF7A, Medium Green #5A9A4A, Warm Cream #F5F2EC, Gray #6B7280. April incorrectly used BridgeWorks navy/gold; May corrects this.
- **Voice:** unchanged from April. Short sentences, specific numbers, no em dashes, no AI slop.
- **Hashtags:** 4-5 per post. Mix one broad + one geo + one industry-specific + one topic-specific. Bilingual: EN versions use English hashtags, HU versions use Hungarian. New in May; April did not have them.
