## Pricing Recommendation

### What to charge CEE FM

**Context:** Budapest market, B2B facility management client, 12+ year company, existing BridgeWorks engagement. CEE FM already pays for the 16-week digital growth retainer.

### Option A: Full Package (Recommended)
**350,000 - 500,000 HUF (EUR 900 - 1,300)**

Includes everything listed above. Delivered in 2-3 weeks.

This is fair for Budapest B2B. A Hungarian design agency would charge 500K-1.2M HUF for comparable work. You deliver faster with AI tooling. The margin is strong.

### Option B: Essentials Only
**200,000 - 300,000 HUF (EUR 500 - 800)**

Includes: Brand guidelines PDF, logo package, social media kit, email signature, and OG image. No print collateral. No deck template.

### Option C: Add-On to Existing Retainer
**150,000 - 200,000 HUF (EUR 400 - 520)**

Position it as an add-on to the current 16-week engagement. Lower price, but locks in goodwill and increases total contract value. Makes sense if CEE FM is price-sensitive.

### Pricing rationale
- Hungarian design agencies charge 300K-800K HUF for brand guidelines alone
- Social media kits run 150K-400K HUF separately
- Business collateral templates: 100K-300K HUF
- You are bundling everything. That justifies a package discount.
- CEE FM is an existing client. They trust you. This is an upsell, not a cold pitch.
- Your cost is near zero (AI-generated). Your value is in the strategy, consistency, and speed.
