## What it is
A step-by-step workflow for using GoHighLevel's new AI Studio feature to build websites, sales pages, and client funnels using AI prompts. No coding required. Jason demonstrates building complete, conversion-focused websites in minutes.

## How it works
1. Access GoHighLevel AI Studio (may need to unlock early access)
2. Give it a simple prompt describing the business and desired page
3. AI generates a professionally designed website with all business info
4. Iterate with conversational prompts ("I like this, change that")
5. Create multiple landing pages with different angles for the same offer
6. Use for client delivery or affiliate marketing pages

## BridgeWorks application
GoHighLevel is a competitor platform to what BridgeWorks builds custom.
