## What it is
A repeatable framework for pitching and delivering AI automation to service businesses (law firms, real estate agencies, HR departments, finance teams). Neuracore AI published 4 videos in 48 hours covering the same core pattern applied to different verticals.

## How it works
1. Identify the "time-sucking vortex" of manual work in the target industry (data entry, document chasing, repetitive communications)
2. Position no-code automation as "digital Lego blocks" the client can understand
3. Start with client intake / lead capture automation as the first win
4. Layer in AI for understanding, reasoning, and communication (beyond simple rules)
5. Show ROI by converting non-billable hours to billable hours (or equivalent metric)
6. Progress from simple rule-based bots to autonomous AI agents that handle multi-stage tasks
