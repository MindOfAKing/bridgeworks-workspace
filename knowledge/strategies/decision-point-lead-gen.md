## What it is
Alex Hormozi's approach to getting fast leads for service businesses. Instead of optimizing lead channels, focus on finding decision points in the customer journey that immediately precede doing business with you, then go to people who already have those customers and make deals with them.

## How it works
1. Map the customer journey for your target client
2. Identify the decision points 1-2 steps before they would hire you
3. Find who already has those customers at that stage
4. Make a deal with that person/business (referral, partnership, commission)
5. Skip cold outreach entirely by intercepting warm demand

## BridgeWorks application
For BridgeWorks: who do SMEs in Budapest talk to right before they need a website or digital presence? Accountants, business formation lawyers, coworking spaces, chamber of commerce events. Build referral partnerships with these decision-point holders rather than cold outreach.
