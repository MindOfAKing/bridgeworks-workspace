## What it is
A mindset framework for using pain and adversity as fuel for business success. Iman argues that most successful people he knows were initially running away from pain rather than toward pleasure. The progression: desperation drives early hustle, then you shift to inspiration, then later you need to tap into "dark motivation" again to push through plateaus.

## How it works
1. Identify the pain or adversity you are running from (not toward pleasure)
2. Use that as fuel for early-stage grinding when motivation is low
3. At a certain success level, shift from desperation to inspiration
4. When you hit a plateau, tap back into the darker motivation to push through
5. Do not villainize this drive. Channel it productively.
