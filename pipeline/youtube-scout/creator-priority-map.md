## Weekly Scout Mix

Default weekly mix:

1. Two AI automation agency creators
2. One sales/offers creator
3. One technical implementation creator
4. One content/authority or SME education creator

If CEEFM or a live client delivery sprint is active, replace the content slot with website, analytics, SEO, or conversion.

## Extraction Bias

Prioritize videos that produce:

- A BridgeWorks service package
- A delivery SOP
- A prospecting angle
- A client-facing explanation
- An automation workflow
- A website or GEO standard
- A reusable report, checklist, or approval brief

Skip videos that only produce motivation, generic AI news, or lifestyle positioning.
