## Sales / Positioning Angles

This protects BridgeWorks from selling automation for problems that need offer clarity, better copy, human sales judgment, or client process cleanup first.

## Audit Preview Improvements

Add an automation-fit row before any automation recommendation:

- Is the task repeated weekly?
- Is the input structured enough?
- Are the rules clear?
- What happens if the automation is wrong?
- Who approves outputs?
- What remains human-owned?
- What proof shows it works?
