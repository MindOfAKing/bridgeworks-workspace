## Useful Ideas

- GEO is not just SEO renamed. Good SEO helps, but AI search gives more weight to brand visibility, mentions, citations, and third-party consensus.
- Links are not the only signal. LLMs can ingest mentions and citations without a direct backlink.
- Query-level source mapping matters. The source set can change by query: website, third-party sites, Reddit, niche blogs, review sources, or retrieval from search.
- AI visibility should not be sold as one magic ranking tactic. It is an authority and evidence system.
- Organic trust may matter more than paid placements in AI surfaces because users come to LLMs for the best answer, not the highest bidder.

## What This Changes For BridgeWorks

This strengthens the AI Visibility Report methodology. BridgeWorks should keep scoring visibility as evidence, not as a promise. Each scan should map the query, answer, cited or implied sources, competitor mentions, owned-site readiness, and third-party proof gaps.
