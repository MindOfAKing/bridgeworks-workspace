## Core Thesis

SEO is now search-everywhere optimization. Traditional search still matters because AI systems use web retrieval, local packs, review sites, social platforms, and third-party citations as raw material. BridgeWorks should turn this into a simple Search Everywhere Visibility Map for Foundation builds and DGE reports.

## Systems And Processes

BridgeWorks Search Everywhere Visibility Map:

1. Define the buyer query.
2. Check traditional Google results.
3. Check local pack and GBP consistency if local intent exists.
4. Check AI answers and citation sources.
5. Mark whether the client appears in the answer, in citations, or on cited pages.
6. Check relevant third-party profiles and review sources.
7. Add focused website, GBP, citation, profile, and proof improvements.
8. Report what changed and what still needs owner confirmation.
