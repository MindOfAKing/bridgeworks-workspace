## Useful Ideas

- Sell a narrow diagnostic before implementation. The transcript frames the core offer as a 45-minute interview for small business owners, then 3 to 7 off-the-shelf AI tools to reclaim 5 to 10 hours per week.
- The first call should probe, not prescribe. Ask about the owner’s day, dreaded tasks, piled-up work, failed automation attempts, and the one process they would delete if they could.
- Use the call transcript as the research object. Feed it to Claude to find pain points and possible tools, then manually QA the recommendations before the client sees them.
- The report should stay simple: executive summary, effort versus impact matrix, recommendations, 4-day quick-start plan, and financial impact.
- Fix the process before automating it. One example reduced a 16-step process to 7 steps before any automation engagement.
