## Core Thesis

Service businesses can lose serious revenue through slow response, weak routing, no follow-up, manual documents, slow invoicing, and missed review requests. The useful BridgeWorks lesson is not the exact revenue claim. It is the audit structure for finding where manual operations leak leads, time, cash, and trust.
