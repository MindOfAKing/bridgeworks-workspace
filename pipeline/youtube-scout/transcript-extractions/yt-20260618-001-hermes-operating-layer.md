## Core Thesis

Hermes becomes valuable when it is used as an operating layer: memory, skills, commands, model routing, integrations, subagents, scheduled work, and dashboards. The mistake is treating it like a one-shot chatbot.

## Systems And Processes

- Level 1: one-shot tasks.
- Level 2: memory, profile, context.
- Level 3: skills, commands, steering, background tasks, model choice.
- Level 4: integrations with email, calendar, meeting notes, task systems.
- Level 5: orchestration through multiple agents/models.
- Level 6: builder plus scheduled asynchronous work.
- Level 7: one AI operating system connecting memory, documents, code, skills, and reporting.
