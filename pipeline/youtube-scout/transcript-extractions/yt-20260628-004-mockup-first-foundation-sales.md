## Core extraction

The useful pattern is not the tool pitch. It is the order of the sales motion:

1. Find visible local website gaps.
2. Build a small proof asset before asking for a meeting.
3. Ask permission to show it.
4. Diagnose the business goal before pricing.
5. Translate features into business outcomes.
6. Offer recurring care only when the client needs ongoing lead capture, updates, reporting, or automation.

## BridgeWorks adaptation

Use this as a `Foundation Mockup-First Sales Loop`:

- Prospect source: Google Maps, Instagram, LinkedIn, local directories, referral partners.
- Fit check: active business, 20 to 100+ reviews where relevant, visible website gap, clear offer, reachable decision-maker.
- Proof asset: one homepage mockup or one audit-preview screen. No full free build.
- Message: short, specific, permission-based.
- Discovery: ask about lost leads, customer value, contact-form flow, booking/order friction, and update burden.
- Package: Foundation site first. Care/automation only if there is a real operational need.
