## Core Thesis

The durable opportunity is not another $20 AI tool. It is repeatable skill engineering: capturing context, examples, workflow steps, error handling, and proof tests so an AI system can perform a real business function again and again.

## Service Fulfillment Lessons

BridgeWorks should not sell "AI agents" as a vague output. It should define the repeated task, context source, approval gate, proof output, and rollback path. If the task cannot be tested with sample inputs, it is not ready.

## Sales / Positioning Angles

A useful line: "We do not automate tools. We automate a specific repeated business function, then prove it works on a sample before touching live operations."
