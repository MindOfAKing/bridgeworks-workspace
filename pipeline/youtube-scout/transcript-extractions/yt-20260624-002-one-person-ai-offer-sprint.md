## Adapted BridgeWorks rule

**Sell-before-build guardrail:** Before creating a new offer asset, define:

1. What result BridgeWorks sells.
2. Who has the pain now.
3. Why the pain is visible from public evidence.
4. What proof can be shown without pretending to have more capacity than exists.
5. Price range and scope boundary.
6. Approval gate before any outreach.
7. Build only after a real signal.

## Guardrails

- Do not copy the aggressive agency claims.
- Do not send LinkedIn or email outreach without Emmanuel approval.
- Do not position BridgeWorks as a staffed agency.
- Do not use AI-generated prospect lists without manual verification.
- Do not charge by hours for Foundation or audit work unless explicitly scoped.
