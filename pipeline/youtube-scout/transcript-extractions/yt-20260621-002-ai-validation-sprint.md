## Problem

Founders build before proving that people want the offer. AI makes building faster, but it can also make wasted building faster.

## Framework

Evidence-led validation sprint:

1. Find a proven demand signal.
2. Ask AI for blue-ocean variants inside a hot category.
3. Convert one idea into a product spec.
4. Generate mockups and screenshots.
5. Build a landing page with a quiz or waitlist.
6. Add conversion tracking.
7. Generate ads.
8. Run a small paid test with a loss limit.
9. Use lowest-cost conversion data to iterate.

## Workflow

For BridgeWorks, adapt this into a low-cost validation sprint:

1. Use public demand evidence, not guessing.
2. Draft an offer or product spec.
3. Build a simple landing page and honest waitlist.
4. Remove fake proof, fake reviews, and inflated waitlist claims.
5. Add tracking only after the page is reviewed.
6. Create 3 to 5 ad variants.
7. Emmanuel manually approves spend and publishing.
8. Review cost per qualified lead, not vanity traffic.
