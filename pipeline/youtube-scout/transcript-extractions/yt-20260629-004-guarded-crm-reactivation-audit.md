## Core Thesis

Many local businesses have old leads, missed calls, past customers, and CRM records that were never followed up. The useful BridgeWorks lesson is not to copy the aggressive SMS pitch. The useful lesson is a guarded reactivation audit: find dormant demand, estimate value, define approval gates, and only then propose a small recovery workflow.

## Systems And Processes

1. Ask whether the business has a CRM or customer list.
2. Confirm consent source and whether SMS/WhatsApp/email follow-up is lawful and appropriate.
3. Segment dormant leads, missed calls, incomplete enquiries, and old customers.
4. Estimate value per appointment or order.
5. Draft one reactivation message with owner approval.
6. Include stop-on-response and manual handoff.
7. Report appointments, replies, opt-outs, and quality guardrails.
