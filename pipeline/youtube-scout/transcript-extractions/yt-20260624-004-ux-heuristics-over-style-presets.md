## Adapted BridgeWorks rule

Add a `heuristics before polish` row to website and app QA:

1. Does the page make the next action obvious?
2. Is hierarchy clear without decorative tricks?
3. Are spacing, contrast, and typography consistent?
4. Does every section earn its place?
5. Does the interface look trustworthy for a small business buyer?
6. Did AI generate a theme, or did it improve a user decision path?
