## Practical framework

1. Find a product or service page.
2. Define ICP, age band, pain point, platform, and desired ad style.
3. Ask the model to research working hooks and formats before writing.
4. Generate 3 to 5 scripts.
5. Select actor / voice by ICP fit.
6. Generate a 10 to 12 second UGC draft.
7. Review like a human. Do not accept first-pass output as final.
8. Only then consider a Meta ad setup, with manual approval and spend limits.
