# BridgeWorks Free Audit Preview Spec

Version: 1.0  
Created: 2026-05-17

## Purpose

The free audit preview is the PDF attached to the first follow-up email after the initial outreach message.

It makes the website promise real without giving away the full implementation plan.

## Positioning

Name:

**BridgeWorks Audit Preview**

Use variants:

- GEO Audit Preview
- Market Audit Preview
- Conversion Audit Preview
- Technical Audit Preview
- Speed-To-Lead Audit Preview

## Length

2 to 4 pages.

Never more than 5 pages for cold outreach.

## Structure

### Page 1: Cover

Include:

- BridgeWorks.agency lockup
- Report type
- Prospect company
- Website reviewed
- Date
- One-line audit thesis

### Page 2: Findings

Include exactly three findings.

Each finding has:

- What I saw
- Why it matters
- Severity: Low, Medium, High

### Page 3: Business Impact

Include:

- Pain versus outcome summary
- Likely effect on enquiries, trust, visibility, or lead quality
- One quick win
- One bigger opportunity

### Page 4: Next Step

Include:

- Recommended BridgeWorks next step
- Suggested call topic
- Simple CTA

## Free Versus Paid Boundary

### Free Preview Includes

- Three findings
- High-level business impact
- One quick win
- Recommended direction

### Full Paid Audit Includes

- Full issue list
- Prioritized implementation roadmap
- Tool/workflow recommendations
- Full technical details
- Competitor or keyword map where relevant
- Page-by-page recommendations
- Build plan and scope

## Report Type Rules

### GEO Audit Preview

Use when:

- Outreach angle is AI visibility gap.
- Prospect lacks structured proof.
- Prospect should appear in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI, or local search.

Include:

- Citability issue
- Schema/content issue
- Proof/authority issue

Hold back:

- Full schema JSON
- Full query set
- Full content plan

### Market Audit Preview

Use when:

- Outreach angle is positioning, category, trust, or competitive gap.

Include:

- Positioning issue
- Competitor or category gap
- Offer clarity issue

Hold back:

- Full competitor matrix
- Full repositioning strategy
- Full messaging rewrite

### Conversion Audit Preview

Use when:

- Outreach angle is website conversion leak.

Include:

- CTA issue
- Proof/trust issue
- Form or lead path issue

Hold back:

- Full wireframe
- Full copy rewrite
- Full CRO test plan

### Technical Audit Preview

Use when:

- Outreach angle is SEO, tracking, performance, sitemap, robots, schema, or metadata.

Include:

- One technical foundation issue
- One tracking or measurement issue
- One search/discoverability issue

Hold back:

- Full remediation checklist
- Exact code snippets
- Full analytics setup

### Speed-To-Lead Audit Preview

Use when:

- Outreach angle is slow response, weak lead routing, no follow-up, or contact form risk.

Include:

- Lead capture issue
- Response path issue
- Follow-up gap

Hold back:

- Full automation diagram
- Full n8n workflow
- Full message sequence

## Follow-Up Email With Attachment

Subject:

```text
Quick audit preview for [Company]
```

Body:

```text
Hi [Name],

Following up on my note about [specific issue].

I put together a short free audit preview for [Company]. It is attached here.

It covers:
- The pain I noticed
- The outcome it may be blocking
- Three things I noticed
- The next step I would recommend

I kept it short on purpose. The full version is better walked through on a call, because not every issue is worth fixing first.

If useful, I can talk you through it this week.

Emmanuel
BridgeWorks.agency
```

## Quality Rule

The report must feel specific enough to earn trust.

If the report could apply to any company, do not send it.

Every report and follow-up email must make the pain versus outcome explicit. The reader should understand both what is leaking now and what better business state BridgeWorks is pointing them toward.
