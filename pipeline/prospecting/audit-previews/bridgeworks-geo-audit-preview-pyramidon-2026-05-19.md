## Audit Thesis

Pyramidon already has unusually strong proof for a property management company: 6,500+ apartments managed, 35+ years of experience, 98% client satisfaction, a named team, direct contact details, and clear Budapest district focus.

The issue is not lack of substance. The issue is that this proof is mostly presented for human readers, not structured for AI systems, answer engines, or local search platforms to confidently cite and recommend the company.

## Finding 1: Strong Proof Exists, But It Is Not Packaged For AI Citation

**What I saw**

The homepage contains strong, specific proof:

- 6,500+ apartments managed
- 35+ years of experience
- 98% client satisfaction
- District III, XI, and XIII service focus
- Named team members with roles and email addresses
- Clear service categories: common representation, accounting, technical supervision, legal consulting, and maintenance coordination

**Why it matters**

AI systems cite concise, self-contained answer blocks. A long homepage can be crawled, but it is harder for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, or Google AI Overviews to extract a clean answer such as:

> Pyramidon is a Budapest property management company managing 6,500+ apartments, primarily in Districts III, XI, and XIII.

**Severity:** High

**Preview recommendation**

Create AI-citable content blocks and dedicated pages for:

- Property management in Budapest
- Condominium common representation
- District III property management
- District XI property management
- District XIII property management
- Team and leadership

---

## Finding 2: Human Trust Signals Need Entity Markup

**What I saw**

Pyramidon has a named team, staff roles, emails, phone numbers, office locations, service explanations, and measurable operating claims.

**Why it matters**

These are valuable trust signals, but AI systems need structured data to connect them into a stable entity. Without Organization, LocalBusiness, Service, BreadcrumbList, FAQPage, and Person schema, the site leaves AI platforms to infer the structure manually.

**Severity:** High

**Preview recommendation**

Add structured data for:

- Organization or LocalBusiness
- Service catalog
- Person profiles for leadership and visible team members
- Office locations
- Contact points
- FAQPage
- BreadcrumbList
- sameAs links to verified third-party profiles

---

## Finding 3: The AI Crawler And Indexing Layer Needs Verification

**What I saw**

The live homepage is accessible and content-rich. The crawler and indexing layer still needs a proper technical pass. In this preview, robots.txt, sitemap coverage, and llms.txt could not be confirmed cleanly.

**Why it matters**

GEO depends on more than readable copy. AI systems need to discover, crawl, interpret, and trust the site. If robots.txt, sitemap, llms.txt, schema, and canonical signals are incomplete, Pyramidon can have strong content while still being under-represented in AI-generated answers.

**Severity:** Medium

**Preview recommendation**

Run a technical GEO pass covering:

- robots.txt and AI crawler access
- sitemap.xml and lastmod coverage
- llms.txt or AI content guide
- canonical and hreflang checks
- schema validation
- Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster signals
