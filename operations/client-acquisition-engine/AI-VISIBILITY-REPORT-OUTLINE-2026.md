# BridgeWorks Small-Business AI Visibility Report 2026

Date created: 2026-07-14
Working size: 12 to 20 pages
Primary CTA: request an AI Visibility Scan.

## Report thesis

Small businesses are losing visibility because AI systems cannot confidently understand who they are, what they do, where they operate, and why they should be trusted. The fix is not generic AI content. It is structured business evidence: entity clarity, schema, crawlability, profiles, reviews, service pages, and citable proof.

## Draft structure

1. Executive summary.
2. What AI visibility means for small businesses.
3. Why normal SEO scores do not tell the whole story.
4. How BridgeWorks evaluates AI visibility.
5. The five-part signal stack:
   - entity clarity
   - structured content and schema
   - crawlability and sitemap access
   - profiles, reviews, and consistent business information
   - proof, service pages, and buyer questions
6. Common problems found in BridgeWorks scans.
7. CEEFM proof: from 16/100 to verified 77/100.
8. Oliviks observations once launch evidence is verified.
9. Practical small-business checklist.
10. What to fix before spending more on ads.
11. Invitation: request an AI Visibility Scan.
