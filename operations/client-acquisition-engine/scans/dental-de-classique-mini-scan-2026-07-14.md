## Commercial Consequence

Patients can discover the clinic through Maps and directories, but there is no controlled destination that explains treatments, clinicians, patient proof, location details, and the next booking step together. That weakens the clinic's ability to turn existing reputation into direct demand and gives search or AI systems fragmented source material.

## Three Actions

1. Launch a fast owned-domain patient-acquisition site with treatment, clinician, location, FAQ, and trust pages.
2. Make phone and confirmed WhatsApp booking actions consistent across the site and public listings.
3. Reconcile address details and publish structured clinic, service, and FAQ data from one controlled source.
