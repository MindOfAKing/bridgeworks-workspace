# Per-Channel Content Notes

Condensed operating notes per channel, derived from
operations/content-channel-model.md and operations/content-90-day-rollout.md.
Use alongside templates/content-item-template.md; this file does not
replace it.

## Personal LinkedIn (brand EE, channel LI)
Role: founder authority. Evidence, informed opinion, lived experience.
Primary distribution engine while the BridgeWorks Page is small.

## BridgeWorks LinkedIn (brand BW, channel LI)
Role: institutional authority. Methods, service explanations, proof,
frameworks, conversion. Depends on founder amplification for now.

## Personal Instagram (brand EE, channel IG)
Role: founder face. Stories, behind-the-scenes, belief, learning.
Selected Collab posts with @bridgeworksagency. Do not duplicate every
business post.

## BridgeWorks Instagram (brand BW, channel IG, @bridgeworksagency)
Role: carousels, Reels, service education, proof, offers. Treat as a
clean launch (1 follower, 0 media as of 2026-07-22).

## Personal Facebook (brand EE, channel FB)
Role: founder commentary, relationship-led amplification. Prefer
sharing a BridgeWorks Page post with a personal introduction.

## BridgeWorks Facebook (brand BW, channel FB)
Role: business education, services, offers, events, proof, conversion.
Active business Page connection exists.

## Nigerian WhatsApp Status (brand EE, channel WA)
Role: manual posting only, from the private Nigerian number. Produce
9:16 assets and captions only. Never store or expose the number. No
API or automation. Record only a manual posted receipt after Emmanuel
confirms.

## Hungarian WhatsApp Business (brand BW, channel WA)
Role: future CEE inbound channel. Preparation only right now: account
checklist, profile requirements, greeting, away message, catalogue
structure, labels, English/Hungarian templates. Do not migrate,
configure, or send automatically.

## X (brand BW, channel X)
Role: reserved. Proposed activation days 31-60 of the rollout, only if
the first month is operationally stable.

## TikTok (brand BW, channel TT)
Role: reserved. Proposed activation days 61-90, only if two vertical
videos per week are sustainable.

## Amplification rule
One origin channel per content unit. The rest amplify with a native
intro or a collab, never a duplicate caption. Record origin and
amplification channels on every content item.
