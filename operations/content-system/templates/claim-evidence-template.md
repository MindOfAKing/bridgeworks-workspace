# Claim/Evidence Template

Fill one of these per claim before it is allowed to move into an
approval card. Per the runbook's "Content involving current clients,
financial results, testimonials, or performance claims requires
supporting evidence" rule and the 2026-07-22 correction pass. No
unsupported quantitative claim may move into an approval card
(templates/approval-card-template.md) without a Claim ID from this
ledger attached.

## Rule

If a claim in a content-item record, approval card, or publishing
receipt does not have a matching, approved Claim ID here, treat it as
unsupported and hold the content item at `Reviewed`, not `Approved`,
until it does. See `registry/claim-evidence-ledger.csv` for the running
index of Claim IDs already issued.
