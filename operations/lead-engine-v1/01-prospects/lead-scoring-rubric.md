# BridgeWorks Lead Scoring Rubric

## Ideal customer profile

Primary: owner-led B2B and local service businesses with higher contract values.

Prioritize:
- facility management
- maintenance
- cleaning
- security
- property services
- clinics and dental
- specialized local services
- consultants/service firms with unclear lead flow

Secondary proof lane:
- restaurants and hospitality, especially where Oliviks can create proof, referrals, or visual examples

## HOT: respond within 2 hours

A lead is HOT when most of these are true:
- Specific need maps to BridgeWorks: enquiries, trust, website clarity, Google presence, follow-up, digital foundation, simple automation, business systems.
- Clear timeline: ASAP, this week, this month, launch date, campaign date.
- Budget signal: mentions budget, asks for proposal, has an operating business with meaningful contract value.
- Fit: Budapest, Hungary, CEE, Nigeria, West Africa, or trusted referral.
- Asks for a call, proposal, audit, or implementation.

Recommended action: reply personally and offer a 20-minute call.

## WARM: follow up within 48 hours

A lead is WARM when:
- Real business, but need is vague.
- Online presence shows visible lead leaks or trust gaps.
- They need education before buying.
- No clear budget/timeline yet.
- Came from LinkedIn, referral, WhatsApp, event, local research, or content engagement.

Recommended action: offer the 48-Hour Lead Leak Review or ask one clarifying question.

## COLD: log only

A lead is COLD when:
- No business identified.
- Low contract value and high support burden.
- Asks for free work or unrelated work.
- Generic copy-paste message.
- No budget, no urgency, no clear need.
- Outside BridgeWorks scope.

Recommended action: log it, maybe send one polite boundary-setting reply.

## Lead leak signals

Look for:
- unclear offer
- weak Google Business Profile or review presence
- no trust proof
- unclear contact path
- slow or hidden enquiry path
- broken forms or poor mobile flow
- no follow-up mechanism
- vague service pages
- no proof of outcomes

## Score fields

Use this format in the tracker:

- Score: HOT / WARM / COLD
- Reason: one sentence
- Recommended offer: 48-Hour Lead Leak Review, website upgrade, digital foundation, AI automation, retainer, diagnostic sprint
- Next follow-up date
- Status: Research / Drafted / Sent / Replied / Call Booked / Proposal Sent / Won / Lost / Nurture
