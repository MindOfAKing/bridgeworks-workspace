# 48-Hour Lead Leak Review: A+ Real Estate

Date: 2026-06-23
Website: https://aplusproperty.hu/
Lane: Property services
BridgeWorks score: **WARM**
Priority: **5**

## Fit

Good fit, especially for investment/property management. Strong property values mean high upside, but the site appears more sales/listing-led than management-led.

## Evidence observed

- Offers buying, selling, renting, investing, property management and renovation.
- Shows high-value listings, including 100m² properties above 200M HUF.
- Has property filters, agent pages, featured properties and testimonials.
- Property management page has a clear form and service claims around tenant screening and online tracking.

## Likely lead leaks

- Property management page is thin and repetitive. It says “Best Property Manager in Budapest” but gives limited proof in extracted content.
- Some visible listing/title errors hurt premium trust, e.g. “FOR SELE”, “TOW BALCONIES”.
- The site splits attention across sales, rentals, investments, renovation and management. Management may not receive a clear conversion path.
- Two repeated “Contact Us For More Details” sections and reCAPTCHA friction may affect form confidence.
- Premium price points need premium copy quality and proof.

## Quick wins

- Fix listing title typos and any property-detail copy errors.
- Expand property management proof: process, response times, owner portal, tenant screening details, testimonials, case examples.
- Create one owner/investor CTA: “Ask us to manage your Budapest property.”
- Separate investor, seller, renter and management journeys more clearly.
- Add trust proof near high-value listings: agent credentials, transaction support, buyer/investor process.

## Outreach angle

Focus on premium trust leakage. High-value listings plus copy errors create a clear reason to review the enquiry path.

## Internal recommendation

Do not send outreach automatically. If Emmanuel approves outreach, send a short message offering 3 practical observations, not a full audit or PDF.
