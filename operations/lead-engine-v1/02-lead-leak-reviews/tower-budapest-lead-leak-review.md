# 48-Hour Lead Leak Review: Tower Budapest

Date: 2026-06-23
Website: https://www.towerbudapest.com/
Lane: Property management
BridgeWorks score: **WARM+**
Priority: **1**

## Fit

Strong. Best benchmark and likely best outreach candidate if message is respectful and not basic.

## Evidence observed

- 800+ properties managed, 15+ years, 90+ multilingual staff, Tower365 owner portal, 24h response promise, named senior property manager.
- Strong proof and testimonials reduce trust risk.
- Broad service range: management, rentals, sales, investments, renovation, accounting.

## Likely lead leaks

- Homepage and service pages contain many competing paths: management, rentals, sales, investment, valuation, Tower365, renovations. A new landlord may not immediately know the one best next step.
- The 24-hour contact promise is strong but could be made more prominent beside the main landlord CTA.
- Switching-owner angle exists but could be sharper: “Already have a manager? See if switching is worth it.”
- Proof is strong but scattered across pages. A concise landlord decision block could improve conversion.
- Some service language feels generic despite strong real assets. The strongest proof should lead earlier.

## Quick wins

- Create one primary CTA for foreign landlords: “Get a 24h property management consultation.”
- Add a short “Switching from another manager?” section near the top.
- Put 800+ properties, 90+ staff, 24h callback, and no take-over fee in one proof strip.
- Add a 3-step path: send property details, receive consultation, switch/activate management.
- Use the named senior manager/contact as a trust anchor on the management page.

## Outreach angle

They are not broken. The useful angle is conversion clarity for foreign landlords who are already comparing managers.

## Internal recommendation

Do not send outreach automatically. If Emmanuel approves outreach, send a short message offering 3 practical observations, not a full audit or PDF.
