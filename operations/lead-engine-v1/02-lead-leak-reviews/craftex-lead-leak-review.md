# 48-Hour Lead Leak Review: Craftex

Date: 2026-06-23
Website: https://craftex.hu/
Lane: Maintenance / renovation / fit-out
BridgeWorks score: **WARM+**
Priority: **3**

## Fit

Strong sub-lane fit. Best if positioned around commercial property owners, investors, offices, clinics, hospitality and fit-out, not generic residential renovation.

## Evidence observed

- Clear service range: construction, renovation, maintenance, fit-out, architecture, interior design.
- Serves homes, businesses and institutions in Budapest.
- Free quote and free consultation offer.
- Contact page has phone, email and form.
- Process is clear: consultation, visit/quote, design, planning, execution, handover.

## Likely lead leaks

- Displayed metrics such as Team Members 0+, Projects Completed 0+, Languages 0+ are credibility leaks if visible live.
- The offer is broad across residential, commercial, institutions, design, architecture and maintenance. Commercial buyers may not see their route quickly.
- Main CTA is “Request a Free Quote”, but there is no visible response-time promise.
- Trust proof needs tightening: project examples, before/after, client types, insurance/permits, testimonials, and commercial references.
- Contact page says “as soon as possible”, which is weaker than a clear 24h or 48h promise.

## Quick wins

- Fix any 0+ counters immediately if visible.
- Create a dedicated commercial maintenance / fit-out landing path.
- Add response promise: “We reply within 1 business day.”
- Add proof blocks by segment: office, clinic, retail, hospitality, property investor.
- Put phone/email plus consultation CTA in a sticky or repeated block on service pages.

## Outreach angle

This is the clearest “visible leak” candidate because credibility counters and broad positioning may be costing quote requests.

## Internal recommendation

Do not send outreach automatically. If Emmanuel approves outreach, send a short message offering 3 practical observations, not a full audit or PDF.
