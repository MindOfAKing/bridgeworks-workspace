# Cold Outreach Templates

Use only after reviewing the business. Mention one specific issue. Do not mass-send generic messages.

I run BridgeWorks, where I help owner-led service businesses improve trust, enquiry paths, and simple follow-up systems.

Would you like me to send 3 quick observations from a 10-minute review?
