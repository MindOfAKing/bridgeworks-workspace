# BridgeWorks Lead Engine v1

Private local pipeline for BridgeWorks client acquisition.

Purpose: find right-fit businesses, score them, send specific outreach, follow up, and move useful conversations toward calls.

Uncomfortable truth: this is not an AI agency machine yet. It is a small, boring sales pipeline. That is the point.

## Focus

### Primary niche

Owner-led B2B and local service businesses with higher contract values.

Examples:
- facility management
- maintenance companies
- cleaning companies
- security services
- property services
- clinics and dental practices
- specialized local services
- consultants or service firms with unclear lead flow

### Secondary proof lane

Restaurants and hospitality.

Use Oliviks for proof, visuals, referrals, and learning. Do not let restaurants define the whole BridgeWorks niche unless the market proves they can pay sustainably.

## Weekly rhythm

- Find 10 businesses.
- Score them.
- Message 3.
- Follow up.
- Book calls.
- Repeat weekly.

## Core offer

**48-Hour Lead Leak Review**

Also usable as: **Lead Leak & Trust Audit**

Promise: I’ll show where your business may be losing enquiries through weak trust signals, unclear contact paths, poor Google presence, or slow follow-up.

Deliverable:
- 1-page review
- 5 priority leaks/fixes
- screenshot notes where useful
- simple next-step recommendation
- optional implementation package

## Build only for now

1. Prospect tracker
2. Lead scoring rubric
3. Audit template
4. Outreach templates
5. Follow-up reminders
6. Weekly review checklist

## Do not build yet

- full agent architecture
- CRM orchestration
- automated scraping machine
- content engine
- complex dashboards

## Use with Hermes

Paste a prospect or website into Hermes and ask:

```text
Use the BridgeWorks Lead Engine v1. Score this prospect, identify likely lead leaks and trust gaps, recommend the best BridgeWorks offer, and draft a short outreach message in Emmanuel's voice.
```

## Guardrails

- No spam.
- No fake personalization.
- No automated publishing/sending without Emmanuel's review.
- Primary lane: owner-led B2B/local service businesses with stronger contract value.
- Secondary proof lane: restaurants/hospitality.
- Keep the offer simple and outcome-based.
