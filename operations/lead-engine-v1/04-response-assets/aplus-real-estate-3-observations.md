## Short reply

Hi A+ Real Estate team,

Thanks for replying. Here are the 3 quick observations I noticed:

1. **Premium listings need premium copy quality.**
   Because some listings are high value, small title errors like “FOR SELE” or “TOW BALCONIES” can reduce confidence before a buyer or investor submits an enquiry.

2. **The property management page needs stronger proof.**
   The page says “Best Property Manager in Budapest”, but the extracted content gives limited proof beside that claim. I would add process, response expectations, owner benefits, and client proof closer to the contact form.

3. **The site serves several buyer journeys at once.**
   Buying, selling, renting, investment, renovation, and management are all valuable, but each visitor needs a clearer next step. Investors and owners should not have to work out which path is for them.

The practical fix is not a full rebuild. I would start with copy cleanup on high-value listings and a sharper owner/investor enquiry path.

Best,
Emmanuel
