# LinkedIn Audit Runbook (Monthly)

Version: 1.0 (proposed)
Created: 2026-07-22
Owner: Emmanuel Ehigbai
Status: Governance runbook. Read-only measurement process. Do not edit LinkedIn, do not auto-post, do not connect accounts. Do not optimize for impressions alone.

Run monthly. Export the data, record it against the date, and read outcomes, not vanity metrics.

## What to export

**Personal profile**

- 365-day content analytics export
- Audience analytics export
- Post-level results
- Profile views
- Search appearances
- Follower growth
- Website and enquiry actions where traceable

**BridgeWorks Page**

- Content analytics export
- Follower analytics
- Visitor analytics
- Search appearances
- Top posts
- Page completeness
- Company-page follower growth

## What to measure

- Impressions
- Reactions
- Comments
- Reposts
- Saves
- Sends
- Profile visits
- Follower growth
- Website clicks
- Qualified conversations
- Calls booked
- Opportunities influenced

Do not optimize for impressions alone. Weight qualified conversations, calls booked, and opportunities influenced above reach.
