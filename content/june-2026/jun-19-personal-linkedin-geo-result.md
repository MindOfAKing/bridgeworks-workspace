My first client scored 16 out of 100 on GEO.

GEO measures how visible a business is to AI search tools. ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews use it to decide what to cite. A score of 16 means the business was invisible to all of them.

They had been operating for 16 years. Real clients. Real contracts. A website that returned nothing useful to any AI crawler.

Over 8 weeks, we built the foundation they were missing.

New website. Structured data across every key page. A complete sitemap. An llms.txt file. Canonical tags. Explicit permissions in robots.txt for AI crawlers. A prospect database of 84 target companies. A Google Ads brief. 29 branded social images.

GEO score at close: 78 out of 100.

+62 points in 8 weeks.

A score of 78 means AI search tools can now find, read, and cite the business. That did not exist before we started.

None of the fixes required advanced technology. All of them were simply missing.

If you run a business in Budapest or Central Europe and your digital foundation has never been audited for AI visibility, your score is likely under 30. Not because the business lacks credibility. Because almost no one has built this infrastructure yet.
